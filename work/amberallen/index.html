Project Intro

Amber Allen Publishing focuses exclusively on personal development, inspirational, spiritual and health related books, e-books and audio books.

As a designer for Amber Allen, I created online marketing collateral as well as digital book covers and illustrations for the various E-books.

Strategy & Concept

One of my more memorable projects was designing for the concept brand Better Life Business, a division of Amber Allen.

The design centered around a tree to symbolize growth and life, a main theme of the company. My first draft of the tree was a very rough outline of what I had in mind. I layed down the lines as quick as possible so that I could keep the image and direction fresh. I took the design further by adding a second tree and highlights to give the illustration some dimensionality.
