Stormpath

Graphic Designer and Front End Developer

Project Intro

Stormpath is a B2B specializing in user management integration with applications. Its hosted API allows developers to quickly and easily add support for authentication and authorization.

As a designer and front end developer for Stormpath, my tasks were wide ranging including maintaining code on the marketing website, developing ad campaigns for digital and print channels to designing trade show collateral such as booth designs and white paper layouts.

Strategy & Concept

One of my top priorities, during my time at Stormpath, was to help the brand mature and grow visually. A key step in this process was to update and lockdown the Stormpath styleguide. I decided that an online guide that was only accessible to team members was the most logical solution. Eventually it could be a platform for other designers and developers to contribute content.
